New Zealand

This oceanic archipelago has grown as a tourist destination for its volcanoes, Edenic beaches and cities with a high standard of living. Auckland is its most populated city with beautiful beaches, jungle coasts and charming bays. Wellington, its capital, stands out for its active nightlife and rich culture. In the town of Whakatane you can visit the Whakaari, an active marine volcano. On the Coromandel Peninsula you will find warm beaches and natural saltwater pools, which are formed at low tide. New Zealand is one of the most geographically isolated countries in the world and its endemic fauna will surprise biodiversity observers.
